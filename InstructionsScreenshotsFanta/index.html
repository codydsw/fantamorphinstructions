Sequence Length

Once you have clicked "length..." in the sequence tab, it will open a new window that allows you to adjust the frame count. Every new morph that is being created will need the sequence length adjusted on both "Sequence 1" and "Sequence 2". Each sequence will have their own unique frame count settings.
